Why we should get a cat

1. Cats are independent and low-maintenance pets.

If they have cat food, water, and a litter box they can be left alone with no heartbreak when you leave for work every day.

You can even leave your cat alone for a full day because cats also don’t require daily exercise walks at all and they don’t need your constant attention, which will let you relax sometimes.

2. Cats cost less money and time to care for

Owning a cat it’s more affordable than owning a dog because cats require less food, fewer toys, and almost no grooming costs.

Adoption fees are usually smaller for cats and kittens, especially during the hot months when shelters tend to get overflowed with kittens.

Cats toys are cheaper and very easy to do at home yourself, plus they would rather sit in a box than in a bed.

DIY cat toys include old cables, hair ties, scrunched-up paper, and bottle caps.
